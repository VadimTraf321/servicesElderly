HOW DO YOU SELECT STAFF?
All caregivers undergo an initial interview. To register with the company, you must provide: a passport, a certificate of fluorography, a health book and other health-related documents. The candidate also provides a certificate of no criminal record, references from a previous place of work, educational documents. If at the time of registration the candidate is missing something, we give directions for the preparation of these documents.
IF I DON'T HAVE MESSENGERS OR EMAIL?
